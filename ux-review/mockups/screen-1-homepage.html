ToCoE

Table of Contents of Everything

A collaborative platform for creating comprehensive, CC0-licensed tables of contents for books, articles, and other written works.

This project is part of the Massively-Multiplayer Online Bibliography (MMOB) initiative, which aims to create a comprehensive, crowdsourced bibliography of all written works through collaborative effort.

Our goal is to produce high-quality tables of contents that can be used for:

  • Academic research and citation analysis
  • Library cataloging and discovery
  • Full-text search and indexing
  • Digital preservation and archiving

All contributions are released to the public domain under CC0.
